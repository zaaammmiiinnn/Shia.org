About Imam and Science

Imam and Science is a knowledge initiative dedicated to exploring and documenting the scientific heritage of Islamic civilization. Our mission is to make this rich intellectual tradition accessible to modern readers.

We provide well-researched articles, historical analyses, and educational resources that highlight the contributions of Muslim scholars to various fields of knowledge throughout history.
